Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy? +
We offer 7-day return policy. Items must be unworn with tags attached.
How long does shipping take? +
Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping available for 2-3 days.
Do you ship internationally? +
Yes, we ship to most countries. International shipping takes 10-15 business days.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept UPI, Credit/Debit cards, and Cash on Delivery.
How do I track my order? +
You'll receive a tracking number via email once your order ships.
